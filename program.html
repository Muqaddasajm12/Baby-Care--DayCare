Graphics Arts

Graphic Arts program is all about sparking creativity and self-expression in young minds.Through hands-on activities like drawing,coloring,painting,crafting, and basic design,children explore shapes,colors,patterns, and visual storytelling. General Science

Our activities are designed to be safe, engaging, and age-appropriate, helping children observe, ask questions, and think like little scientists. Whether they’re planting seeds, watching ice melt, or exploring the five senses, kids are encouraged to explore how things work in a playful, guided way. With General Science, we build the foundation for future critical thinkers — one exciting discovery at a time.
